Build Directions

Ravenswatch builds are decided by the talents you pick during a run. This shows, for each hero, which build each one's talent pool actually supports: the keyword themes, how many talents feed them, and which are available from the start. Talent data from the Ravenswatch Wiki.

These are derived from the talent data, not a hand-picked meta list: the game writes its mechanics as keywords in the talent text, and the wiki's own new-player guidance is to keep taking talents that share a keyword with what you already have. A theme is listed when at least 3 of a hero's 26 talents feed it. For full community builds with items, use the official Ravenswatch Build Maker.
